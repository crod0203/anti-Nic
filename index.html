The real poison is Vapes.

E-cigarette use has become common, but there still seems to be a smoke screen concealing its true impact on health. A growing body of evidence shows that smoking e-cigarettes, or vaping, may be even more dangerous than smoking cigarettes. Vaping is the inhaling of a vapor created by an electronic cigarette or other vaping device.Vaping has become one of the more popular ways to consume both tobacco and marijuana. It consists of an atomizer, a power source such as a battery, and a container such as a cartridge or tank. Instead of smoke, the user inhales vapor.
